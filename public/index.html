Frequently Asked Questions

Is GrabbVideos free to use?

Yes — 100% free, unlimited, no account required. Just paste, grab, and download.

Does it remove the TikTok watermark?

Yes. GrabbVideos fetches the original source video directly — the downloaded MP4 has no TikTok watermark.

What Instagram content can I download?

Reels, video Posts, and IGTV from public accounts. Private accounts are not accessible.

What video quality will I get?

GrabbVideos always selects the best available MP4 quality — up to HD/1080p, matching the original upload.

Do I need to install anything?

No. 100% browser-based. No app, no extension, nothing to install.

Is my data safe?

We do not store or share URLs. All processing is real-time and discarded immediately after the download. See our Privacy Policy.

Is it legal to download Instagram or TikTok videos?

Downloading for personal, offline viewing is generally accepted in most regions. Never re-upload content you don't own without the creator's permission, as that may infringe copyright. Always respect each platform's terms of service.

Can I download videos from a private account?

No. GrabbVideos can only access public content. Private profiles are protected and cannot be fetched without authentication, keeping user privacy intact.

Does GrabbVideos work on iPhone and Android?

Yes. The site is fully responsive and works in any mobile browser — Safari on iOS, Chrome on Android, Firefox, and more. No app or extension required.

Why do I get "Could not grab this video"?

The most common causes are: the account is private, the video has been deleted, or the URL was incomplete. Tap Share → Copy Link directly in the app to get the full URL, then try again.
